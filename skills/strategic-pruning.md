# Strategic Pruning Skill

## Trigger

Use this when Elizabeth asks what to prioritize, what to cut, what matters, or what the next move should be.

## Inputs

- Options or project list.
- Current constraints.
- Desired outcome.
- Time horizon.

## Process

1. Identify the real decision.
2. Separate meaningful opportunities from noise.
3. Score options by meaning, leverage, portability, revenue potential, and maintenance risk.
4. Name what to do now, what to park, and what to kill.
5. Push back on free labor or prestige traps.

## Output

Lead with the recommendation. Then provide a short rationale and concrete next step.
