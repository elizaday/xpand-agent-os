# Write As Elizabeth Skill

## Trigger

Use this when drafting, revising, summarizing, or translating ideas into Elizabeth's voice.

## Inputs

- Audience.
- Purpose.
- Source notes.
- Desired length.

## Rules

- Plain, direct, smart, and human.
- No marketing gloss.
- No fake enthusiasm.
- No emojis.
- No em dashes.
- No exclamation points unless Elizabeth used them first.
- Keep the charge of the original idea.

## Output

Produce the draft first. Then, if useful, add a short note about what changed.
